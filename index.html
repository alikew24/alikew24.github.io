About Me

I am a high school student with a passion for computer programming.

Ever since I was very young, I have had a curious passion for how things work. From solving abstract problems in high school math competitions to creating web applications, I have always been passionate about analyzing complex problems and finding the most optimal, efficient solutions to them. I graduated from The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania, where I majored in Finance and Economics. I find that Finance and Software Development are similar in that they both involve deconstructing projects into manageable pieces and having a meticulous attention to detail. Since college, I have enjoyed building innovative, full-stack web applications that provide the user with a customized and interactive experience.
